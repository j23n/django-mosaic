This could be your little home on the internet.

Mosaic is a project that helps you claim your own space on the internet. It's supposed to be yours, away from social media and tech giants.

It integrates well with the IndieWeb movement, which is all about normal people like you building their homes on the internet. The three guiding principles of the IndieWeb are: your content is yours, you are better connected, you are in control.

Mosaic supports:

  • webmention
  • some microformats (h-entry)
  • RSS feeds
  • A private space for private posts

Mosaic is not supposed to be a one-and-done thing. The spirit of the IndieWeb is also understanding of the technology. As such, an effort has been made to keep the code base accessible and easy to understand.

If you're using it to build your website, please send us a link - we'd love to hear about it!
